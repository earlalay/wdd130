Different Kinds of Dinosaurs

Different kinds of dinosaurs ate different things. Some only ate plants, some only ate meat, and some ate both!

Dinosaurs that only ate plants are called herbivores. The ones that only ate meat are called carnivores. And the ones that ate both are called omnivores.

A famous group of herbivores are the sauropods! They're the ones with long necks and tails. A famous group of carnivores are the theropods, like T-Rex!
